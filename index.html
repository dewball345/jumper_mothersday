Jumper

A mother's day game for my mom, Suma

Rules:

  • Click or press space to jump
  • You can "stand" on green tiles but you cannot run into them from the side.
  • Also, if you fall from a long distance, you MAY lose. This is technically a bug but i'll call it a feature
  • Your score is the amount of time you stay afloat. If you lose, a nice message will await you
